Divisibility Rules

Divisible by 2 If the last digit is even (0, 2, 4, 6, 8).
Divisible by 4 If the number formed by the last two digits is divisible by 4.
Divisible by 5 If the last digit is 0 or 5.
Divisible by 8 If the number formed by the last three digits is divisible by 8.
Divisible by 3 & 9 If the sum of the digits is divisible by 3 (or 9).
Divisible by 10 If the last digit is 0.
Divisible by 11 Find the sum of digits in odd positions and the sum of digits in even positions. If the difference is 0 or divisible by 11, the number is divisible by 11.
e.g., 294708480: Odd Sum=26, Even Sum=15, Diff=11.
